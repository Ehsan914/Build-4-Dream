Privacy Policy for Shade of the Palm Ltd

At Shade of the Palm Ltd, we are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy and protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and secure your data when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect:

We may gather and process the following types of data:

  • Information you provide by filling out forms on our website, such as when you request a quote or contact us.
  • Records of your communications with us, including emails, phone calls, or other forms of interaction.
  • Details of transactions carried out through our website or when using our services.
  • Data collected through cookies and similar technologies during your visit to our website. Please refer to our Cookie Policy for more details.

2. How We Use Your Information:

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To deliver the services you request, including providing quotes, estimates, and completing projects.
  • To communicate with you about our services, updates, and promotions.
  • To manage and improve our website, services, and customer relationships.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

3. Disclosure of Your Information:

We may share your personal data with:

  • Trusted service providers and contractors who assist us in delivering our services.
  • Legal and regulatory authorities, if required by law or to protect our rights, property, or safety.
  • Third parties in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of part or all of our business.

4. Data Security:

We implement appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, please note that no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights:

If you have an account on this site or have left comments, you can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you, including any data you have provided. You may also request that we delete any personal data we hold about you, except for data we are required to keep for administrative, legal, or security purposes.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy:

We may revise this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy regularly.

7. Contact Us:

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please contact us at contact@shadeofthepalm.co.uk.

Policy Commitment

Shade of the Palm Ltd is committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our clients and website visitors. We adhere to stringent data protection laws and industry best practices to ensure the security and confidentiality of all entrusted information. We are dedicated to transparency in how we collect, use, and safeguard your data and continuously review and update our policies and procedures to uphold the highest privacy and data security standards.
